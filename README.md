# ⚡ Smart Grid Commander

A futuristic smart-city power grid simulation game built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Smart Grid Commander allows players to manage renewable energy generation, battery storage, city demand, and grid stability in a dynamic futuristic city environment.

---

# 🚀 Features

## 🌆 Futuristic Smart City
- Animated city skyline
- Glowing buildings
- Power flow animations
- Modern game-inspired UI

---

## ⚡ Energy Management System
- Solar power generation
- Wind energy generation
- Dynamic city electricity demand
- Battery storage management

---

## 🌦 Dynamic Weather Engine
Weather directly affects energy production:
- Sunny ☀
- Cloudy ☁
- Windy 🌬
- Storm 🌩

---

## 🧠 AI Grid Assistant
Smart AI alerts help monitor:
- grid stability
- blackout risks
- demand spikes
- emergency situations

---

## 🚨 Emergency Events
Random events create gameplay challenges:
- industrial overloads
- transformer failures
- grid instability

---

## 💰 Economy System
- Earn credits by maintaining stable power
- Upgrade solar farms
- Upgrade battery systems
- Improve grid performance

---

## 🏙 Population Growth
City population changes dynamically depending on:
- grid stability
- energy availability
- system efficiency

---

## 🌑 Blackout Simulation
Low stability causes:
- blackout effects
- darkened buildings
- emergency warnings

- HTML5

# 🎮 Gameplay

The objective is to maintain a stable smart-grid system while managing renewable energy sources and growing the futuristic city.

Players must:
- balance energy generation
- manage battery levels
- respond to emergencies
- prevent city-wide blackouts
